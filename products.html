Questions

Frequently asked

Is LITE really free forever?
Yes. LITE is free with no time limit, no credit card required and no hidden costs. We believe every maker deserves to know their real costs before spending money on software.
Is this a subscription?
No. PRO and XL are one-time payments. You pay once and own the license forever, including all future updates for your tier. No recurring charges.
Can I upgrade from LITE to PRO later?
Yes. You can download LITE now and upgrade at any time. Your data stays intact when you upgrade — just enter your license key and all features unlock instantly.
Does the app work offline?
100% offline. PrintNeat is a Windows desktop app — your data never leaves your computer. No internet connection required to use any feature.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If PrintNeat isn't right for your business within 14 days of purchase, contact us and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
How many PCs can I install it on?
Each license covers 1 PC. If you need to transfer to a new machine, just contact support and we'll help you move your license within minutes.
Will prices stay the same?
No — launch pricing is active now and prices will increase as PrintNeat grows and more features are added. If you buy today, you lock in the current price and get all future updates for your tier.
